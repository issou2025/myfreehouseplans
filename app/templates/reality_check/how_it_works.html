How it works

This is a prevention system against unfinished projects. It does not calculate prices.

1) Surface increases stress

Big projects last longer. That increases fatigue, coordination load, and the chance of running out near the end.

2) Levels multiply complexity

Height adds time and coordination. More levels means more moments where the project can become fragile.

3) Country adjusts pressure

We may adjust risk slightly based on context pressure (not cost). If unknown, we use a neutral default.

What the zones mean

  • Green: strong chance of completion — if you keep scope stable.
  • Orange: possible, but fragile — protect the finish and avoid expansions.
  • Red: high risk — reduce surface/levels or split into finishable milestones.
